Synthesis.GraphViz

Description

Pretty-prints a dataflow graph to DOT (GraphViz). Nodes are rendered as rounded boxes with short mnemonics, and edges are labeled with (sourcePort,targetPort). Edges are sorted primarily by destination id, then by destination port number, which tends to produce stable layouts.
